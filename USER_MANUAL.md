# TourDesk — User Manual

## Overview
TourDesk is a travel discovery and booking platform. This manual explains how to use the application as an end user.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the App
Open the app in your browser at the deployed URL (or `http://localhost:3000` when running locally).

### Browsing Places
1. On the home page, use the search bar to find cities or attractions.
2. Click **Places** in the navbar to browse all available destinations.
3. Use the filter options to narrow results by category or city.

### Booking a Tour
1. Click on any place card to view details.
2. Click the **Book Now** button.
3. Fill in your name, email, date, and number of guests.
4. Submit the form — you will receive a confirmation message.

### Viewing Your Bookings
1. Click **My Bookings** in the navbar.
2. All your past and upcoming bookings are listed here.

### AI Assistant
1. Click the **AI** widget in the bottom-right corner.
2. Ask the assistant questions like "What are the best places in Hyderabad?"
3. The assistant can recommend places based on your preferences.

## Settings
- **Language**: Use the language selector in the navbar to switch between supported languages.
- **AI Provider**: In the AI settings modal, you can configure your preferred AI backend and API key.

## Troubleshooting
| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Page does not load | Ensure the backend is running on port 8000 |
| Booking fails | Check that all required fields are filled correctly |
| AI does not respond | Verify your API key is set in AI Settings |
